Electric Utilities

PG&E, SCE, PacifiCorp, SDG&E, Hawaiian Electric, European utilities. The combination of aging grid infrastructure in WUI terrain and strict liability exposure creates an existential financial risk for utilities. ForestSat provides the intelligence layer for mitigation planning, execution verification, and regulatory reporting.

Products for Utilities

  • Insight™ — Map fuel accumulation along transmission corridors and identify highest-risk spans
  • Guardian™ — Plan and execute vegetation management programs along rights-of-way
  • MVR™ — Independent satellite verification of vegetation management execution for AB 1054 compliance and CPUC reporting
  • Response™ — Integrate with PSPS (Public Safety Power Shutoff) decision-making systems
  • Recovery™ — Post-fire damage assessment and subrogation evidence support

Products for Insurance

  • MVR™ — Verifiable risk reduction evidence for premium discounts and policy conditions
  • Recovery™ — Objective satellite-based damage assessment for claims processing and subrogation support
