The Strategic Problem

Suppression Is Failing. Prevention Is the Answer.

"The 2% of fires that escape initial attack consume more than 30% of the total suppression budget. They cannot be stopped by firefighting — only by weather changing."
USFS · National Interagency Fire Center data

Portugal spent €6.585 billion on fire suppression and only €410 million on prevention between 2000–2017 — a 16:1 ratio. The result: 117 deaths in a single year. The US Forest Service's budget for fire has grown from 16% in 1995 to over 50% today. The LA 2025 fires triggered a $6.55B federal budget request — a 245% increase in one year.

The science is clear: every $1 invested in prevention saves $6–12 in suppression. But prevention only works when institutions know where to act, what to fund, and whether it worked. That is ForestSat's purpose.
